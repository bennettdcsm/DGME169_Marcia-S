Beach Boys: 3D Character and Environmental Art

A case study focused on character creation, organic modeling, and the construction of a whimsical outdoor environment using a comprehensive Blender-only workflow.

Final render demonstrating character concept and beach environment.

Design Goal

To create a fun, expressive character and place them in a believable, tropical outdoor scene, focusing on stylized PBR materials.

Tools & Workflow

  • 3D Software: Blender
  • Key Techniques: Organic Modeling, UV Unwrapping, Scene Lighting.

Skills Showcased

Cartoon character design, environmental composition, creating realistic sand/water materials, and posing for narrative effect.

The Blender-Only Workflow

1. Character Modeling & Sculpting

The dog character was modeled and sculpted entirely in Blender. This involved block-out modeling for the base shape, followed by sculpting tools to define the face, fur detail, and cartoon proportions.

2. Environmental Modeling & Texturing

The scene, including the sand, an sky were built and textured within Blender. Custom node setups were utilized to create the subtle sandy texture and the clear, sunny sky giving the environment depth.

3. Composition and Final Render

The final stage involved lighting the scene using an HDRI and several fill lights to achieve the bright, mid-day aesthetic. Composition was key, carefully placing the lobster and adjusting the camera lens to create a playful, dynamic interaction between the character and the viewer.
